Who We Are

Sawasdee Budget Vacations is a small travel agency based in Bangkok that has spent more than ten years sending travelers across Thailand on a shoestring. Our local guides and trip planners grew up here and know how to stretch every baht.

Our Travelers

We work with students, young professionals, and families who want real Thailand — street food, temples, islands, night trains — without luxury-hotel prices. If you care more about mango sticky rice than marble lobbies, you’re our people.

Why Thailand

Thailand is one of the easiest and most affordable countries in Asia for first-time travelers. Excellent public transport, guesthouses for every budget, and world-class beaches mean you can enjoy a two-week adventure for less than a weekend in many Western cities.
