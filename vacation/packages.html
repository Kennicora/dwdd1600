Sample Thailand Packages

Every trip is customized, but here are three sample packages to give you an idea of pricing, hotels, and what’s included. Prices are approximate and based on double occupancy.

Bangkok & Chiang Mai Starter

8 Days / 7 Nights — from $899 per person

Begin in buzzing Bangkok with a temple tour, canal boat ride, and night market visit. Then head north to Chiang Mai for mountain temples, a cooking class, and a day at an ethical elephant sanctuary.

Destinations: Bangkok, Chiang Mai

Hotel (Bangkok): Hotel Clover Asoke

Hotel (Chiang Mai): Maladee Rendezvous Hotel

Rental car: Compact car with Avis Thailand

Approximate cost: $899 + international airfare

Andaman Island Hopper

10 Days / 9 Nights — from $1,150 per person

Fly into Phuket, explore nearby islands, then continue to Railay and Krabi for kayaking and cliffside viewpoints.

Destinations: Phuket, Railay, Krabi

Hotel (Phuket): Katathani Phuket Beach Resort

Hotel (Railay): Rayavadee Resort

Hotel (Krabi): Aonang Villa Resort

Rental car: Economy rental with Hertz

Approximate cost: $1,150 + international airfare

Classic Thailand Loop

14 Days / 13 Nights — from $1,650 per person

Bangkok, Ayutthaya, Chiang Mai, and the southern islands — a perfect balance of culture, food, and relaxing beach days.

Destinations: Bangkok, Ayutthaya, Chiang Mai, Koh Tao or Koh Samui

Hotel (Bangkok): Centre Point Silom Hotel

Hotel (Chiang Mai): De Chai The Deco Chiang Mai

Hotel (Koh Tao): The Decks Koh Tao

Rental car: Optional rental with Budget Car Rental

Approximate cost: $1,650 + international airfare
