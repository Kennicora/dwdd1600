Essential Gear for Rappelling

Protective Gear

  • Helmet – Protects from falling rocks and bumps.
  • Gloves – Prevent rope burn and improve grip.
  • Footwear – Shoes with sticky rubber soles for traction.
  • Clothing – Lightweight, breathable, and abrasion-resistant.

Core Equipment

  • Harness – Secures the rappeller to the rope system.
  • Ropes – Static ropes commonly used for rappelling.
  • Carabiners – Connectors for gear and rope.
  • Anchors – Bolts, webbing, or natural features.

Rappel Devices

  1. ATC – Versatile belay/rappel device.
  2. Figure 8 – Smooth control during descent.
  3. GriGri – Assisted-braking device for added safety.
  4. Pirate – Friction-control carabiner for rappelling.

Glossary

Carabiner
A metal loop with a gate used to quickly connect components.
Harness
A system of webbing used to safely attach to the rope.
Static Rope
Low-stretch rope designed for rappelling.
