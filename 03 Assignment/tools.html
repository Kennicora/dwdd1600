Essential Gear for Rappelling

Protective Gear

  • Helmet – protects from falling rocks and bumps
  • Gloves – prevent rope burn and improve grip
  • Footwear – shoes with sticky rubber soles for traction

Core Equipment

  • Harness – secures the rappeller to the rope system
  • Ropes – static ropes commonly used for rappelling
  • Carabiners – connectors for gear and rope
  • Anchors – bolts, webbing, or natural features to secure the rope

Rappel Devices

  • ATC – versatile belay/rappel device
  • Figure 8 – smooth control during descent
  • GriGri – assisted-braking device for added safety

Backup & Safety

  • Prusik Knot – backup friction hitch
  • Ascenders – devices for climbing the rope if needed
  • Extra Webbing – for building anchors or replacing worn material
